    \section{Kirchoff's Laws}

    \subsection{Kirchoff's Current Law}

    For all lines in and out of a junction: \begin{equation}
        \sum{I} = 0
    \end{equation}

    \subsection{Kirchoff's Voltage Law}

    Around any closed loop circuit: \begin{equation}
        \sum{V} = 0
    \end{equation}
